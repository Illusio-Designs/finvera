### **1.1 Total Revenue Report**
**What it shows:**
- Total lifetime revenue (all time)

### **1.2 Revenue Comparison Report**
**What it shows:**
- Period vs Period comparison (e.g., This Month vs Last Month, This Year vs Last Year)
- Revenue growth/decline percentage
- Side-by-side comparison table
- Visual charts showing differences
- Best performing periods

**Data inside:**
- Selected period 1 (from-to dates)
- Selected period 2 (from-to dates)
- Revenue for period 1
- Revenue for period 2
- Difference amount and percentage
- Growth trend indicators
- Plan-wise comparison

**Data inside:**
- Revenue by acquisition category (distributor, salesman, referral, organic)
- Revenue by plan (FREE, STARTER, etc.)
- Revenue by commission type
- Count of tenants per category
- Average revenue per category
- Visual pie charts and bar charts

- Monthly revenue for selected period

## ðŸŽ¯ **3. PERFORMANCE REPORTS** (3 Reports)

### **3.1 Distributor Performance Report**
**What it shows:**
- List of all distributors
- Performance metrics per distributor:
  - Total tenants acquired
  - Total revenue generated
  - Total commissions earned
  - Target achievement percentage
  - Active vs inactive status
- Top performers ranking

**Data inside:**

- Performance rating/ranking

---

### **3.2 Salesman Performance Report**
**What it shows:**
- List of all salesmen
- Performance metrics per salesman:
  - Total tenants acquired
  - Total revenue generated
  - Total commissions earned
  - Target achievement percentage
  - Conversion rate (if leads tracked)
- Top performers ranking
- Performance comparison chart

**Data inside:**
- Salesman name, code
- Associated distributor (if any)
- Number of tenants acquired
- Revenue generated
- Commissions earned
- Targets set vs achieved
- Achievement percentage
- Performance rating/ranking

---

### **3.3 Target Achievement Report**
**What it shows:**
- All active targets (distributor + salesman)
- Target vs Achieved comparison
- Achievement percentage
- Targets by type (revenue, subscription)
- Targets by period (monthly, quarterly, yearly)
- Over-achievers and under-achievers

**Data inside:**
- Target ID, type, period
- Target owner (distributor/salesman name)
- Target value
- Achieved value
- Left to achieve
- Achievement percentage
- Status (on track, behind, exceeded)
- Date range
